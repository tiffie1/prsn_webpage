Latency graphical showcase

This webpage was made with the purpose of developing a graph showcasing the latency of NTRIP stations.
The latency values given are static, and do not represent the actual latency of the stations.



Port Reading Test:

It has been decided that port reading is too complicated for this project. As such, it will be replaced with file reading instead.



Flexbox / Char.js Oragnization:

A visual test of organization via flexbox and Chart.js. A search bar has yet to be included, and there will be a total of 30 graphs, one for each avaible station, as shown by the PRSN NTRIP Caster.
You may right click on the graph to open it as an image in a new tab.


Term Explanations:

- Mount Point: The codename of the station.

- IP Address: The internet protocol code of the station.

- Identifier: A short descriptor of the station's location.

- Maximum Latency: The estimated maximum amount of latency a given station can potentially have.

- Minimum Latency: Similar to maximum latency, with the difference of being for the estimated minimum.

- Average Latency: The calculated average of the mean latency from a station.

- Root Mean Square: The calculated estimate of fluctuation of a station's latency. The higher the number, the more likely a station's latency is to fluctuate.
